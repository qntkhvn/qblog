1 About The Project

This is the Final Project Portfolio for my Introduction to Data Science course (Data 229) at Wittenberg University. Data 229 is the first ever Data Science course offered at Wittenberg University and is taught by Professor Douglas Andrews.

My portfolio consists of analysis of six datasets, in two different parts:

  • Part I: Three datasets selected by Professor Andrews (who also wrote the introduction to all 3 datasets):
  1. KenKen
  2. Solar
  3. Dolphins
  • Part II: Three datasets chosen by me:
  1. Goals
  2. Grades
  3. Tennis2017
